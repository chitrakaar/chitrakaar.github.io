Services Offered

Rinse and Refill

We will effectively clean your bottles with our highly pressurized ozonating water cleaning system to ensure sanitization. Once cleaned, we will refill them with our purest, cleanest and mineral rich water that is ready to be taken home or to your business.

Alkaline water

Alkaline water is high pH drinking water produced by adding antioxidant minerals to the purified water.
